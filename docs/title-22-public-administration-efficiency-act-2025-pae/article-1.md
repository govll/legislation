#### Preamble:

To streamline bureaucratic processes, eliminate redundancies, and ensure
transparent, citizen-centric governance under Chapter 3, Article 8
(executive duties) and Chapter 6, Article 17 (civic responsibilities).

### Article 1: Standardized Procedures

#### Section 1A:
All government agencies must adopt a Unified Digital Portal
for licenses, permits, and public inquiries.

#### Section 1B:
Processing time for citizen requests capped at 14 business
days; delays incur fines (e.g., 1,000 Dimes/day).
