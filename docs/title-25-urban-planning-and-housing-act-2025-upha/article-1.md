#### Preamble:

To ensure affordable housing, sustainable cities, and equitable land use
under Chapter 6, Article 16(1) (equality).

### Article 1: Housing Quotas

#### Section 1A:
 20% of new developments reserved for low-income families.

#### Section 1B:
 Tax incentives for eco-friendly architecture (e.g., solar
panels, green roofs).
