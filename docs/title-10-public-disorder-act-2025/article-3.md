### Article 3: Disorderly Conduct

#### Section 3.1:
Disorderly conduct, including public fighting, drunken
behavior, or other disruptive actions in public spaces, is prohibited.

#### Section 3.2:
Individuals engaging in disorderly conduct will face
penalties including fines, community service, or imprisonment for up to
1 year, depending on the severity of the conduct
