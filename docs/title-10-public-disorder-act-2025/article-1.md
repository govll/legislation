### Article 1: General Provisions

#### Section 1.1: 

This Act applies to all acts of public disorder, including
but not limited to harassment, violent protests, public disturbances,
and riots.

#### Section 1.2: 
Public disorder is defined as any action or behavior that
disrupts public peace, safety, or order, endangers the welfare of the
public, or causes harm to individuals or property.
