Article 8: Penalties and Enforcement

Section 8.1: Penalties for offenses under this Act may include fines,
imprisonment, community service, or other rehabilitative measures.

Section 8.2: Penalties will vary based on the severity of the offense,
the harm caused to the public, and the intent of the individual(s)
involved.

Section 8.3: Law enforcement agencies shall be responsible for the
investigation, arrest, and prosecution of offenders under this Act.
