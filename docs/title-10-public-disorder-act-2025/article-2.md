### Article 2: Harassment and Public Disturbances

#### Section 2.1:
It is an offense to engage in any act of harassment or
intimidation towards individuals in public spaces. Harassment includes
verbal threats, unwanted physical contact, or causing distress through
threatening behavior.

#### Section 2.2:
Individuals found guilty of harassment in a public setting
shall be subject to a fine, imprisonment for up to 6 months, or both.

#### Section 2.3:
Public disturbances that affect public tranquility, such as
excessive noise, vandalism, or blocking public pathways, shall also be
penalized.
