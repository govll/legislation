Article 5: Public Safety and Law Enforcement

Section 5.1: Law enforcement officers have the authority to disperse
crowds, arrest individuals, and use reasonable force to restore order
during incidents of public disorder.

Section 5.2: Any law enforcement officer who uses excessive force or
violates the rights of individuals during the enforcement of this Act
shall be subject to disciplinary action.
