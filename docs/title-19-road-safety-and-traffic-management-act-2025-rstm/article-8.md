Article 8: Enforcement

Section 8A -- Traffic Police Authority

\(a\) Officers may issue fines, impound vehicles, or arrest offenders
for severe violations.

Section 8B -- Speed Cameras and Surveillance

\(a\) Evidence from cameras is admissible in court; fines mailed to
vehicle owners.
