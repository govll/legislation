Article C: Constitutional Compliance

Section C1 -- Public Safety

\(a\) Speed limits and DUI laws uphold Chapter 6, Article 16(4) (\"Right
to security\").

\(b\) Penalties align with Chapter 6, Article 16(4) (\"No excessive
punishment\").

Section C2 -- Due Process

\(a\) Traffic courts operate under Chapter 5, Article 14 (\"Independent
judiciary\").
