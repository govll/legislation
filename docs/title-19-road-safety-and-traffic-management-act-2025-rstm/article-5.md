Article 5: Special Zones and Restrictions

Section 5A -- School Zones

\(a\) Speed cameras and traffic wardens enforce 20 mph limits during
school hours.

\(b\) Double fines for violations in school zones.

Section 5B -- Construction Areas

\(a\) Drivers must obey reduced speed limits and detour signs;
non-compliance risks 5,000 Dimes fine.
