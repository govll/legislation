Article 7: Traffic Accident Protocol

Section 7A -- Immediate Actions

\(a\) Drivers must stop, assist injured parties, and report accidents to
police within 24 hours.

\(b\) Fleeing the scene (hit-and-run): 5 years imprisonment and
permanent license revocation.

Section 7B -- Insurance Claims

\(a\) Uninsured drivers are personally liable for damages and face
10,000 Dimes fine.
