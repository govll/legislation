### Article 6: Family and Community

#### Section 6A -- Parental Responsibility

\(a\) Parents/guardians must provide care, education, and protection for
minors.

\(b\) Violations may trigger intervention by the Royal Family Bench.

#### Section 6B -- Duty to Report Crimes

\(a\) Witnesses to violent crimes must alert authorities; failure may
result in fines up to 10,000 Links Landic Dimes.
