Article 4: Contractual Obligations

Section 4A -- Binding Agreements

\(a\) Written or verbal contracts meeting offer, acceptance, and
consideration are enforceable.

\(b\) Fraudulent contracts are void ab initio (from the outset).

Section 4B -- Breach of Contract

\(a\) Remedies include compensation, specific performance, or
termination, adjudicated by the Civil Claims Tribunal or High Chancery
Court of Equity.
