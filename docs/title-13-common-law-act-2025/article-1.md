#### Preamble:

To enshrine foundational legal principles rooted in reason, justice, and
international consensus, ensuring clarity, fairness, and harmony with
the Constitution of Links Land (Chapter 6, Article 16).

### Article 1: General Principles

#### Section 1A -- Reasonableness Standard

\(a\) All laws, actions, and judicial decisions shall adhere to the
standard of reasonableness, balancing individual rights and public
interest.

\(b\) Courts shall interpret ambiguities in favor of fairness and common
sense.

#### Section 1B -- Presumption of Innocence

\(a\) In criminal matters, no person shall be deemed guilty without
proof beyond reasonable doubt.

#### Section 1C -- Good Faith

\(a\) Contracts, agreements, and negotiations must be executed in good
faith; malicious intent voids enforceability.
