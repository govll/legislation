Article 3: Public Awareness

Section 3A -- Education Campaigns

\(a\) Government-funded programs educate parents on content risks and
parental controls.

Article C: Constitutional Compliance

\(a\) Age ratings uphold Chapter 6, Article 16(2) (right to education).

\(b\) The Media Standards Commission operates under Chapter 3, Article
8(2) (executive authority).
