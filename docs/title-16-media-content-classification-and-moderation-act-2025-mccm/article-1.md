#### Preamble:

To establish age-appropriate content standards and regulate media
distribution, ensuring alignment with Chapter 6, Article 16(2) of the
Constitution (\"Right to education and cultural integrity\").

### Article 1: Content Ratings

#### Section 1A -- Classification System

\(a\) All media (films, games, publications) must display age ratings:

U (Universal), PG (Parental Guidance), 15+, 18+.

\(b\) Ratings are assigned by the Media Standards Commission.

#### Section 1B -- Restricted Content

\(a\) Distribution of excessively violent, pornographic, or
discriminatory material to minors is prohibited.
