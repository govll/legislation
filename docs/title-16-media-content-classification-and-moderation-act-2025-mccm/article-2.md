### Article 2: Enforcement

#### Section 2A -- Penalties for Non-Compliance

\(a\) Selling 18+ content to minors incurs fines up to 50,000 Links
Landic Dimes.

\(b\) Repeat offenders face license suspensions.

#### Section 2B -- Streaming Services

\(a\) Platforms must implement parental controls for age-restricted
content.
