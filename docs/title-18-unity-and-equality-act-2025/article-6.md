### Article 6: Conversion Practices Ban

#### Section 6A -- Prohibition

\(a\) \"Conversion therapy\" targeting LGBTQIA+ individuals is banned
for all ages (penalties: up to 10 years imprisonment).

\(b\) Advertising or profiting from such practices is illegal.

#### Section 6B -- Support for Survivors

\(a\) Survivors receive free counseling and legal aid through the Equity
Commission.
