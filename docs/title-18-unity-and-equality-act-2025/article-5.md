Article 5: Cultural and Ethnic Equity

Section 5A -- Language Rights

\(a\) Public services must provide translation support for minority
languages spoken by ≥5% of the local population.

\(b\) Schools in ethnically diverse regions teach at least one minority
language.

Section 5B -- Heritage Protections

\(a\) Sacred sites, cultural practices, and traditional lands of
indigenous groups are legally protected.
