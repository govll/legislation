Article 2: Cybercrime Prevention

Section 2A -- Cyberbullying

\(a\) Harassment, doxxing, or threats online are punishable by up to 5
years imprisonment.

\(b\) Schools must adopt anti-cyberbullying policies.

Section 2B -- Fraud and Hacking

\(a\) Unauthorized access to systems or data theft carries penalties up
to 10 years imprisonment.
