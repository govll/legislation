Preamble:

To regulate digital platforms, combat cybercrime, and protect users'
rights online, per Chapter 6, Article 16(3) of the Constitution (\"Right
to privacy and expression\").

Article 1: Platform Obligations

Section 1A -- Content Moderation

\(a\) Social media companies must remove illegal content (e.g., hate
speech, CSAM) within 48 hours of reporting.

\(b\) Failure to comply results in fines up to 500,000 Links Landic
Dimes per violation.

Section 1B -- Data Privacy for Minors

\(a\) Collecting data from users under 16 requires parental consent.

\(b\) Breaches involving minors' data trigger fines up to 1 million
Links Landic Dimes.
