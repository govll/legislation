### **Article 3: Voting Rights and Education**

#### Section A: Voter Eligibility

1.  All tax-paying citizens and residents aged 16 or older shall be
    eligible to vote.

2.  Political education shall be mandatory in schools from age 10 in
    order to qualify for the electoral roll.

#### Section B: Disqualifications

1.  Any individual charged with three or more High Offences shall be
    permanently disqualified from voting.
