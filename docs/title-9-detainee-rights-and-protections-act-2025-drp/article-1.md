#### Preamble:

Recognizing the supremacy of the Constitution of Links Land (Chapter 6,
Article 16) and the principles of dignity, due process, and justice,
this Act enshrines rights for detainees in accordance with the judiciary
and governance framework of the Constitutional Monarchy.

### Article 1: Right to Humane Treatment

#### Section 1A:

All detainees shall be treated with respect and dignity, free from
torture, cruel, inhuman, or degrading treatment.

#### Section 1B:

Detainees must receive adequate food, water, and sanitation, as defined
by the Public Health and Safety Code.
