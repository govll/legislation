### Article 4: Right to Protection from Arbitrary Detention

#### Section 4A:

No detention shall occur without lawful cause, as defined in Article
1A--1C of the Criminal Proceedings Act 2025.

#### Section 4B:

Detainees must be informed of charges in writing and brought before the
Fine Offence Tribunal, Low Offence Court, or High Offence Court within
48 hours for a hearing.
