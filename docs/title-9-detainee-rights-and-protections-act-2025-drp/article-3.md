Article 3: Right to Communication

Section 3A:

Detainees may communicate with family, legal representatives, or
consulates, subject to security restrictions approved by the Magisterial
Infraction Chamber (for fine offenses) or Low Offence Court (for Low
Offenses).

Section 3B:

Telephone access must be granted within 24 hours of detention.
