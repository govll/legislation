Article C: Constitutional and Legal Alignment

Judicial Safeguards:

All detainee appeals align with Chapter 5 (Judiciary) and Chapter 6
(Rights) of the Constitution.

The Constitutional Court retains authority to nullify detention
practices violating Article 19(2).

Monarchy's Role:

The monarch's ceremonial assent applies to this Act per Chapter 2,
Article 6(1).

Enforcement:

The Speaker of the Democracy oversees parliamentary reviews of detention
policies under Chapter 3, Article 11(1).
