#### **Article 1: County Administration**

1.  Each county shall establish a County Council, consisting of elected
    representatives.

2.  The County Council shall:

- Create local policies and regulations in accordance with national
  laws.

- Oversee the implementation of public services.

- Manage local budgets and development plans.
