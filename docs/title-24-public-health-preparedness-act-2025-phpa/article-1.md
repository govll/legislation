#### Preamble:

To fortify healthcare systems against pandemics, natural disasters, and
bioterrorism, per Chapter 6, Article 16(3) (right to health).

### Article 1: Resource Stockpiling

#### Section 1A:
Mandate 6-month reserves of critical medicines and PPE.

#### Section 1B:
Penalty for stockpile mismanagement: Low offence (fines up
to 100,000 Dimes).
