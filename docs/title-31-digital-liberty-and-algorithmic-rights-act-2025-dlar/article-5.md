Article 5: Enforcement and Oversight

Section A: Digital Rights Commission

The Linkslandic Digital Rights Commission (LDRC) shall be established
to:

Monitor compliance by platforms and agencies

Investigate algorithmic discrimination

Advise Parliament on evolving digital policy

Section B: Civil Redress

Any citizen whose content or identity has been unjustly restricted may:

File complaints directly with the LDRC

Seek legal remedy through the civil courts under the Civil Proceedings
Act 2025
