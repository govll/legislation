Article 4: Digital Education and Culture

Section A: Digital Citizenship in Schools

Schools shall introduce courses on:

Free speech in the internet age

Algorithmic bias and misinformation

Ethics of content moderation

Section B: Community Engagement

Public libraries, churches, mosques, and civil groups are encouraged to
host:

Roundtables on free expression

Workshops on secure and ethical digital communication
