### Article 5: Appeals

#### Section 5A -- First Appeal

\(a\) High Offence Court rulings appeal directly to the Crown Sovereign
Court (Supreme Court).

\(b\) Low/Fine Offence rulings appeal to the High Offence Court.

#### Section 5B -- Final Appeal

\(a\) The Crown Sovereign Court is the final authority for all criminal
appeals.
