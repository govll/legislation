### Article 4: Investigative Powers

#### Section 4A:

The National Security Committee (NSC) may:

Require sworn testimony from officials suspected of treason.

Temporarily suspend recording exemptions in private offices during
investigations.

Freeze assets of individuals accused of treason pending trial.

#### Section 4B:

All investigations require prior approval from the Constitutional Court
to prevent political abuse.
