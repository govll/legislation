Article 3: Criminalization and Penalties

Section 3A:

Treason: Punishable by life imprisonment.

Subversion: Punishable by 15--30 years imprisonment.

Failure to Report: Public officials who knowingly conceal treasonous
acts face up to 5 years imprisonment.

Section 3B:

Exemptions for whistleblowers acting in good faith under Title 26,
Article 2A (EACA).
