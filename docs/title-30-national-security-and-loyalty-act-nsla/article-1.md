Preamble:

To safeguard the constitutional monarchy, democratic institutions, and
territorial integrity of Links Land, this Act criminalizes treason,
subversion, and covert actions aimed at overthrowing the government, per
Chapter 1 (sovereignty), Chapter 7 (military neutrality), and Chapter 6
(civic responsibilities) of the Constitution.

Article 1: Definitions

Section 1A:

Treason: Any act to unlawfully overthrow the government, collude with
foreign powers to harm Links Land's sovereignty, or provide material
support to entities seeking to destabilize the constitutional order.

Subversion: Covert actions to incite rebellion, sabotage critical
infrastructure, or manipulate government processes to undermine lawful
authority.

Loyalty Oath: A sworn declaration by all public officials and military
personnel to uphold the Constitution and report treasonous activities.
