### Article 5: Appeals

#### Section 5A -- First Appeal

\(a\) Civil Claims Tribunal rulings appeal to the High Chancery Court of
Equity.

\(b\) Specialized Court rulings appeal to the Crown Sovereign Court.

#### Section 5B -- Final Appeal

\(a\) The Crown Sovereign Court resolves constitutional conflicts or
precedent disputes.
