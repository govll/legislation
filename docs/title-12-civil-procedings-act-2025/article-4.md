Article 4: Judgments and Enforcement

Section 4A -- Monetary Judgments

\(a\) Enforceable via asset liens, wage garnishment, or property
seizure.

Section 4B -- Contempt Penalties

\(a\) Non-compliance with court orders may result in fines (≤50,000
Links Landic Dimes) or imprisonment (≤30 days).
