Article 2: Commencing Proceedings

Section 2A -- Filing Requirements

\(a\) Claims must specify jurisdiction (e.g., High Chancery Court, Civil
Claims Tribunal).

\(b\) Improperly filed claims are dismissed or transferred within 14
days.

Section 2B -- Counterclaims

\(a\) Defendants may file counterclaims within 30 days of service.
