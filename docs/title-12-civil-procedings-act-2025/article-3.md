Article 3: Evidence and Mediation

Section 3A -- Mandatory Mediation

\(a\) Claims \<100,000 Links Landic Dimes require mediation before
trial.

\(b\) Mediation outcomes are binding if both parties agree.

Section 3B -- Discovery

\(a\) Discovery requests in High Chancery Court require court approval
if contested.
