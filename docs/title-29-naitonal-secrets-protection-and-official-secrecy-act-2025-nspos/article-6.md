Article 6: Enforcement and Penalties

Section 6A -- Espionage

Sharing secrets with foreign entities: Death Penalty or life
imprisonment (if constitutional).

Espionage during wartime: Automatic Death Penalty.

Section 6B -- Negligence

Leaving classified materials unsecured: Low Offence (1--5 years
imprisonment + 50,000 Dime fine).
