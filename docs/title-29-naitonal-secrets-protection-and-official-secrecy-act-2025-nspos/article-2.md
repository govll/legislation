Article 2: Security Clearances and Access

Section 2A -- Clearance Tiers

Level 3 (Confidential): Basic access for entry-level roles.

Level 2 (Secret): Mid-tier for policy advisors, mid-ranking officials.

Level 1 (Top Secret): Reserved for senior officials, military
commanders, and intelligence operatives.

Section 2B -- Vetting Process

Background checks by the National Security Bureau (NSB) include:

Financial history

Foreign contacts

Psychological evaluation

Clearances renewed every 3 years or after foreign travel.
