Article 3: Handling of Classified Information

Section 3A -- Storage and Transmission

Physical Documents: Stored in NSB-approved safes; tracked via barcode.

Digital Data: Encrypted using military-grade algorithms; air-gapped
systems for Top Secret.

Verbal Briefings: Conducted in SCIFs (Sensitive Compartmented
Information Facilities).

Section 3B -- Unauthorized Disclosure

Accidental Breach: Immediate reporting mandatory; penalties depend on
severity.

Intentional Leaks: Treated as High Offence (10--25 years imprisonment).
