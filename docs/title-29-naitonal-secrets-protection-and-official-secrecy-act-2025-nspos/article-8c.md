### Article C: Constitutional Compliance

#### Section C1 -- Rights Balancing

Secrecy protocols respect Chapter 6, Article 16(1) (freedom of speech)
but prioritize national security under Chapter 1, Article 2.

Whistleblower protections uphold Chapter 6, Article 16(4) (right to
justice).

#### Section C2 -- Judicial Oversight

The Constitutional Court reviews all secrecy-related prosecutions to
prevent abuse.
