### Article 5: Funding and Public Grants

#### Section A: Budget Allocation

Parliament shall annually allocate dedicated funds to the National Parks
Authority to cover:

* Park maintenance and protection programs;

* Restoration and rehabilitation projects;

* Public education and tourism services.

#### Section B: Community Participation Grants

Recognized NGOs, community councils, and schools may apply for
conservation and education grants through the NPA to support local
engagement in park preservation.

#### Section C: Financial Oversight

All grants and expenditures shall be subject to annual audit and must
comply with the Financial Regulations Act 2025 (FRA).
