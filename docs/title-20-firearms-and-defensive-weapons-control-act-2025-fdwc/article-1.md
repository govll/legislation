#### Preamble:

To minimize firearm-related harm, ensure responsible ownership, and
permit lawful self-defense under strictly regulated conditions,
balancing public safety with individual rights.

### Article 1: Definitions

#### Section 1A -- Firearm Types

\(a\) Permitted: Semi-automatic handguns (≤10-round capacity), shotguns,
bolt-action rifles.

\(b\) Prohibited: Automatic weapons, high-capacity magazines (\>10
rounds), armor-piercing ammunition, explosives.

#### Section 1B -- Defensive Weapons

\(a\) Includes pepper spray, tasers, and non-lethal stun devices.
