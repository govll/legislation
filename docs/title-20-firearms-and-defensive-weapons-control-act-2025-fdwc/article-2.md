Article 2: Licensing and Eligibility

Section 2A -- Licensing Process

\(a\) Applicants must:

Be ≥21 years old.

Pass a background check (no violent crime history, domestic abuse, or
mental health adjudications).

Complete a 30-hour training course (safety, marksmanship, legal use).

Provide two character references.

\(b\) Licenses expire every 3 years; renewal requires updated checks and
training.

Section 2B -- Self-Defense Permits

\(a\) Concealed carry permits require an additional 10-hour
de-escalation course and proof of secure storage.

\(b\) Permits revoked for any criminal conviction or restraining order.
