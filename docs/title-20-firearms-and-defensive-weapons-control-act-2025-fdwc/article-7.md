### Article 7: Enforcement and Penalties

#### Section 7A -- Inspections

\(a\) Police may conduct random storage inspections for licensed owners.

#### Section 7B -- Trafficking and Illegal Sales

\(a\) Unlicensed sales or modifications: High offence (20 years
imprisonment).

#### Section 7C -- Straw Purchases

\(a\) Purchasing a firearm for an ineligible person: High offence (15
years imprisonment).
