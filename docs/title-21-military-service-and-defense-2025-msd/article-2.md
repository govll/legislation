### Article 2: Military Conduct and Ethics

#### Section 2A -- Oath of Service

All personnel swear allegiance to the Constitution, not individuals or
political parties.

#### Section 2B -- Prohibited Actions

War crimes, torture, or unlawful orders are forbidden under the Criminal
Proceedings Act 2025.

Discrimination based on gender, ethnicity, religion, or sexual
orientation is punishable by discharge (per Unity and Equality Act
2025).

#### Section 2C -- Rules of Engagement

Use of force must comply with international law (Geneva Conventions) and
parliamentary mandates.

Civilian casualties must be minimized; violations trigger investigations
by the Military Tribunal.
