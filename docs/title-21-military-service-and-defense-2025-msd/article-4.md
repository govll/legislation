Article 4: Military Justice

Section 4A -- Military Tribunal

A civilian-supervised tribunal adjudicates violations of military law.

Defendants retain the right to appeal to the Constitutional Court (per
Judiciary and Dispute Resolution Act 2025).

Section 4B -- Penalties

High Offences: Treason, war crimes, or desertion in combat -- up to life
imprisonment.

Low Offences: Insubordination or negligence -- demotion, fines, or
discharge.
