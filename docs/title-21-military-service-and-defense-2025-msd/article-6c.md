### Article C: Constitutional Compliance

#### Section C1 -- Sovereignty and Democracy

Military actions align with Chapter 1, Article 1 (national sovereignty)
and require parliamentary approval (Chapter 4, Article 12).

The Monarch's ceremonial role complies with Chapter 2, Article 4.

#### Section C2 -- Rights and Freedoms

Prohibitions on discrimination uphold Chapter 6, Article 16 (equality).

Conscientious objection exemptions respect Chapter 6, Article 16(3)
(freedom of belief).

#### Section C3 -- Judicial Oversight

The Military Tribunal operates under Chapter 5, Article 14 (judicial
independence).
