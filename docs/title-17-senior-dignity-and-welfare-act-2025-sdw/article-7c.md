### Article C: Constitutional Compliance

#### Section C1 -- Rights and Equality

\(a\) Protections under Article 2A align with Chapter 6, Article 16(1)
(\"Right to dignity and non-discrimination\").

\(b\) Healthcare guarantees (Article 3A) reflect Chapter 6, Article
16(3) (\"Right to health\").

#### Section C2 -- Judicial Fairness

\(a\) Guardianship oversight (Article 5A) ensures due process under
Chapter 6, Article 16(4).

#### Section C3 -- Institutional Authority

\(a\) The Senior Welfare Authority operates under Chapter 3, Article
8(2) (executive enforcement powers).
