Article 3: Healthcare and Accessibility

Section 3A -- Right to Quality Care

\(a\) Seniors have priority access to state-funded healthcare, including
geriatric specialists and home care services.

\(b\) Hospitals and clinics must provide age-friendly facilities (e.g.,
ramps, hearing aids).

Section 3B -- Mental Health Support

\(a\) Free counseling and dementia care programs are available through
the National Health Service.
