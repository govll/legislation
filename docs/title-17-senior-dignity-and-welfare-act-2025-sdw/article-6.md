Article 6: Community and Family Support

Section 6A -- State-Funded Caregiver Programs

\(a\) Families caring for seniors at home receive tax incentives and
respite care subsidies.

Section 6B -- Senior Community Centers

\(a\) Municipalities must operate centers offering social activities,
meals, and health screenings.
