### Article 4: Social Inclusion and Autonomy

#### Section 4A -- Anti-Discrimination

\(a\) Denying seniors employment, housing, or services based on age is
prohibited (fines up to 50,000 Links Landic Dimes).

\(b\) Public transport and infrastructure must prioritize senior
accessibility.

#### Section 4B -- Participation in Civic Life

\(a\) Polling stations and public forums must provide accommodations
(e.g., seating, large-print materials).
