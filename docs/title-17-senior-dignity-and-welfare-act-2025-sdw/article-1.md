Preamble:

To uphold the rights, autonomy, and well-being of senior citizens in
Links Land, ensuring their dignity, safety, and full participation in
society, as enshrined in Chapter 6, Article 16(1) of the Constitution
(\"Equality and freedom from discrimination\").

Article 1: Definitions

Section 1A -- Senior Citizen

\(a\) A \"senior citizen\" is defined as any individual aged 65 years or
older.

Section 1B -- Exploitation

\(a\) \"Exploitation\" includes financial manipulation, emotional
coercion, neglect, or abuse targeting seniors.
