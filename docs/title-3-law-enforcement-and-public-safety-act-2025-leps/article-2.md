#### **Article 2: Police Powers**

1.  Local police officers shall have the authority to:

- Conduct arrests, searches, and seizures as authorized by law.

- Issue citations for minor offenses.

- Enforce traffic regulations and public safety measures.

1.  Police misconduct shall be investigated by an independent oversight
    body.
