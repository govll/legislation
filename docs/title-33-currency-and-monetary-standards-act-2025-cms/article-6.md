### Article 6: Public Confidence and Financial Integrity

#### Section A: Transparency Requirements

The Ministry of Global Stability shall publish an annual report
detailing:

* Total money supply in circulation,

* Inflation targets and trends,

* Economic performance in relation to the Đime.

#### Section B: Financial Education

National financial literacy programs shall be coordinated in schools and
public institutions to promote responsible use and understanding of the
currency system.
