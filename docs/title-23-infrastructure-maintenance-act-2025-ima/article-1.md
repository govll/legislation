Preamble:

To ensure the upkeep of public infrastructure, including roads,
utilities, and digital networks, per Chapter 6, Article 16(3) (right to
public services).

Article 1: Maintenance Standards

Section 1A: Roads repaved every 5 years; utilities inspected quarterly.

Section 1B: Fines for contractors failing safety standards: Up to
500,000 Dimes.
