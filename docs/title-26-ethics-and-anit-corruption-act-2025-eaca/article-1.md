#### Preamble:

To enforce integrity in public office, per Chapter 6, Article 17 (civic
responsibilities).

### Article 1: Financial Transparency

#### Section 1A:
 All officials must publicly disclose assets annually.

#### Section 1B:
Gifts over 1,000 Dimes reported to the Anti-Corruption
Bureau.
